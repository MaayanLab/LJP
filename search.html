From this page you can submit gene sets, or gene signatures, for analysis with the LJP-BCNB. The submitted gene sets and signatures will be projected onto the network of single drug perturbations applied to the six breast cancer cell lines. Clicking on the grey buttons will fill the input boxes with demonstrative examples of the input format. You can also fetch gene expression sigantures we computed from the Cancer Cell Line Encyclopedia (CCLE) as well as 670 disease signatures extracted from the Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO).
